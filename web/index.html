🥊 How do we pick the best?

Every tool fights in the Ring. We ask 6 simple questions: Do people love it? Is it alive? Is somebody taking care of it? Do others build on it? Will a normal human understand it? And most important — can you run it with one click? Add it up and you get the Grandmaster Score out of 100. The strongest tool in each arena wears the crown 👑 — and loses it the moment something better shows up. No ads, no pay-to-win. Just the board.
