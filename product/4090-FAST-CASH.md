## STEP 1 — Your first render (tonight, ~15 min)
You need ONE thing: a clean cutout of a product on a nice background. Take any product
photo (yours, or a free stock one, or a screenshot of a cheap Amazon product) and run
one of these in ComfyUI (Flux). Pick a product image → these are the money recipes:

**A) Clean white studio shot (the bread-and-butter, every seller needs it)**
```
professional product photography of [PRODUCT] on a seamless pure white studio background,
soft even softbox lighting, subtle reflection beneath, crisp focus, high detail, e-commerce
catalog style, 1:1 square, shot on Hasselblad, commercial quality
```

**B) Lifestyle scene (sells for 2–3× the white shot)**
```
[PRODUCT] styled on a warm minimalist [wooden table / marble counter / linen surface],
soft natural window light, shallow depth of field, a few tasteful props ([coffee, plant,
book]), cozy premium lifestyle aesthetic, editorial product photography, 4:5 vertical
```

**C) In-hand / in-use (highest converting for ads)**
```
close-up of a hand holding [PRODUCT], natural skin tones, soft daylight, blurred cafe
background, authentic UGC style, shot on iPhone, 9:16 vertical, realistic
```

Render 3 variations. Upscale with Real-ESRGAN. **That's your portfolio — 3 finished shots.**

> Tip: to transform a REAL product photo (keep the exact product, change the background),
> use a Flux img2img / inpaint or an IPAdapter workflow — ComfyUI-Manager installs the nodes
> in one click. For the first night, generating a similar product from scratch is fine — it's
> just to show buyers what you can do.

You want sellers with UGLY phone photos and money on the line. Where they cluster:

1. **Etsy** — search a category (e.g. "handmade candle", "leather wallet", "resin coaster").
   Sort by **newest**. New shops = worst photos + eager to grow. Note 15 shops with weak photos.
2. **Instagram** — search a niche hashtag (#smallbusiness #shopsmall #handmade[thing]).
   Small shops (500–5k followers) posting phone pics = perfect. Note 10.
3. **Meta Ad Library** (facebook.com/ads/library) — free, public. Search a product keyword,
   filter to your country. Anyone listed is **actively paying for ads right now** = has budget
   and needs creative. Note 5 — these are your best leads.

Put all 30 in the tracker (see `outreach-tracker.csv`).

**Instagram/Etsy DM — opener:**
```

```

**The free-sample close (this is what wins):** actually make the free shot for the ONE

```
Here's [product] cleaned up ✨ Want the full set? 5 photos $15, or 10 for $25, delivered
in 24h. I can do lifestyle scenes too — those tend to boost sales.
```

**Cold email (for Ad Library brands):**
```

```

ever want a batch for a launch or ad, I'm one message away 🙌
